## Full Description (4000 characters max)
Welcome to e-sbe, your ultimate digital catalog and order management companion. Designed for seamless b2b and personal usage, e-sbe simplifies the way you browse products and manage orders.

**Key Features:**

*   **Comprehensive Catalog:** Instantly access a vast array of products from top brands like Cubix, Florex, Paragon, and General Items. Browse through intuitively organized categories to find exactly what you need.

*   **Rich Product Details:** Make informed decisions with high-quality product images and detailed descriptions. Zoom in to see the fine details of every item.

*   **Easy Ordering:** Add items to your cart with a single tap. Review your selections, adjust quantities, and add custom notes for specific requirements.

*   **Flexible Sharing:** Share your orders the way you want. Generate professional PDF documents for formal orders or share quick summaries directly via WhatsApp.

*   **Offline Capability:** Work without interruptions. Your synchronized product data is available even when you're offline.

*   **Admin Tools:** (For authorized users) update stock images and manage product details directly from the app.

Streamline your workflow with e-sbe. Download now to experience a smarter way to manage your catalog and orders.
